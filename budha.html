Vegan Budha Bowl

Make this healthy week-night option

This is the ultimate quick and healthy go to meal. Make this vitamin boosting treat in just 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 3 x ripe avocados
  • A selection of leafy greens
  • 2 tbsp of Tahini
  • 3 tbsp of Hummus
  • 1 beetroot
  • 4 carrots

Method

  1. First, boil the rice in a saucepan until cooked.
  2. Cut the carrots into stips, brush with oil, season and bake for 15 minutes in the centre of a pre heated oven (180c)
  3. Wash and prepare leafy greens.
  4. Next, cut the avocado into small slices.
  5. Layer each component into the bowl.
  6. Add, hummus and tahini to dress.
  7. Season with salt and pepper.
